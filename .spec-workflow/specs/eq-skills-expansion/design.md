# Design: EQ Skills Expansion

## 1. Retaliation Module (Additions)
### 1.1 Theory
-   **Lesson 04: Setting Boundaries (The "Broken Record")**
    -   Technique: Repeat your boundary calmly without explaining or getting angry.
    -   Example: "I'm not discussing this." -> "But why?" -> "I'm not discussing this."

### 1.2 Exercises
-   **Ex 04: Passive-Aggressive Colleague**
    -   Prompt: Colleague says "Must be nice to leave early" (at 5 PM).
    -   Response: "Yes, it is nice. See you tomorrow." (Agree and ignore the sting).

### 1.3 Scenarios
-   **Scenario 03: Public Humiliation**
    -   Context: Someone makes a joke at your expense in a group.
    -   Goal: Call it out without losing cool ("Can you explain why that's funny?").

## 2. Influence Module (New)
### 2.1 Theory
-   **Lesson 01: Reciprocity (Give to Get)**
    -   Concept: People feel obliged to give back.
-   **Lesson 02: Social Proof (Everybody's Doing It)**
    -   Concept: We look to others to guide our behavior.
-   **Lesson 03: Scarcity (FOMO)**
    -   Concept: We want what we can't have.

### 2.2 Exercises
-   **Ex 01: Sales Pitch (Scarcity)**: Sell a pen using scarcity.
-   **Ex 02: Asking for a Favor (Reciprocity)**: Ask a colleague for help after helping them.
-   **Ex 03: Group Decision (Social Proof)**: Convince friends to eat at a restaurant.
-   **Ex 04: The "Because" Justification**: Use "because" to increase compliance.

### 2.3 Scenarios
-   **Scenario 01: Salary Negotiation**: Convince boss for a raise.
-   **Scenario 02: Project Pitch**: Sell an idea to the team.
-   **Scenario 03: Convincing a Friend**: Get a friend to join a gym.

## 3. Anti-PUA Module (New)
### 3.1 Theory
-   **Lesson 01: Gaslighting Detection**
    -   Concept: They make you question your reality ("I never said that").
-   **Lesson 02: The Gray Rock Method**
    -   Technique: Become boring and unreactive to toxic people.
-   **Lesson 03: The Art of Saying No**
    -   Technique: "No" is a complete sentence.

### 3.2 Exercises
-   **Ex 01: Spotting Gaslighting**: Identify the gaslighting phrase.
-   **Ex 02: Gray Rock Practice**: Respond boringly to drama.
-   **Ex 03: Firm No**: Refuse a request without over-explaining.
-   **Ex 04: Guilt Trip Defense**: Handle "If you loved me, you would..."

### 3.3 Scenarios
-   **Scenario 01: Toxic Relationship**: Partner accuses you of being "too sensitive".
-   **Scenario 02: Workplace Bullying**: Boss constantly changes requirements and blames you.
-   **Scenario 03: Peer Pressure**: Friends pressuring you to drink/spend.
