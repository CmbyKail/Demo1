# Requirements: EQ Skills Content Expansion

## 1. Overview
Expand content for three key EQ modules: "High EQ Rebuttal" (Retaliation), "Influence & Persuasion" (Influence), and "Anti-Manipulation" (Anti-PUA). The goal is to provide comprehensive training material including theory, practice, and real-world application.

## 2. Scope
### 2.1 Retaliation Module (Update)
-   **Current Status**: Partially implemented (3 Lessons, 3 Exercises, 2 Scenarios).
-   **Requirement**: Expand to at least 10 items total.
-   **Additions**: Add more advanced techniques for setting boundaries and handling passive-aggressiveness.

### 2.2 Influence Module (New Content)
-   **Current Status**: Empty.
-   **Requirement**: Create full content with at least 10 items.
-   **Topics**:
    -   Core principles of persuasion (Reciprocity, Social Proof, Authority).
    -   Storytelling for influence.
    -   Negotiation basics.

### 2.3 Anti-PUA Module (New Content)
-   **Current Status**: Empty.
-   **Requirement**: Create full content with at least 10 items.
-   **Topics**:
    -   Identifying manipulation (Gaslighting, Guilt-tripping).
    -   Setting firm boundaries.
    -   The "Gray Rock" method.
    -   Refusal techniques.

## 3. Content Structure per Module
Each module must contain:
-   **Theory Lessons**: 3-5 lessons explaining core concepts.
-   **Exercises**: 3-5 interactive exercises (Fill-in-the-blank, Rewrite, Multiple choice).
-   **Scenarios**: 2-4 role-play scenarios with AI evaluation.

## 4. Success Criteria
-   Each module has >10 distinct content items (Lessons + Exercises + Scenarios).
-   Content is culturally relevant and practical.
-   UI renders all new content correctly (no "undefined" errors).
